# Workout plan template

Goal: a simple plan that’s easy to run and easy to progress.

## Weekly structure (example)

- Day 1: Lower (strength)
- Day 2: Upper (strength)
- Day 3: Conditioning + core
- Day 4: Lower (hypertrophy)
- Day 5: Upper (hypertrophy)

## Progression

- Add reps until you hit the top of the range.
- Then add a small amount of weight and repeat.
