# Welcome

This is my AI discussion space: notes, experiments, and longer-form writing as I learn.

What you can expect here:

- Practical writeups (what worked, what didn’t, and why)
- Small experiments (prompting, evaluation, tooling)
- Ideas I’m actively testing

If you want this to be interactive, enable **GitHub Discussions** on this repo and set a link in `site.config.js` (`aiDiscussionsUrl`).
